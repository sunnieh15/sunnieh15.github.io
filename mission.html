Mission & Vision

Mission

"To convince the unconvinced to be convinced that there is redemption from sin and abundant life ON EARTH even into the afterlife through our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ." - John 10:10

Vision

"We will engage in the process of in-reach to create a non-threatening atmosphere of love, effective ministries to serve the present age, outreach to fulfill the Great Commission, and holistic education to build the totality of humankind." - Matthew 28:19-20
