Message From Our Pastor

Thank you for your interest in Antioch Baptist Church. Antioch wants to make a lasting difference in your life, in our community, and in the world by leading people to become fully devoted followers of Christ. We are a Bible believing and teaching church filled with the Holy Spirit, and our congregation is a friendly group of people who enjoy coming together to praise our Lord. Our mission is "to convince the unconvinced that there is redemption from sin and abundant life ON EARTH even into the afterlife through our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ" (John 10:10)

On our website you will find information about who we are and what you can expect when you come to Antioch Baptist Church. So come join us for the Antioch experience. We know you will find our church family to be one that enthusiastically embraces newcomers and warmly welcomes them into our fellowship. We look forward to meeting you and getting to know you better!
