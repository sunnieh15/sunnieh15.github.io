Message From Our Pastor

Thank you for your interest in Antioch Baptist Church! Antioch wants to make a lasting difference in your life, in our community, and in the world by leading people to become fully devoted followers of Christ. On our website you will find information about who we are and what you can expect when you come to Antioch Baptist Church. In addition, you can watch our sermons online. Join us for a weekend service. We look forward to meeting you and getting to know you better!
